About KDocAI - Document Intelligence Solution

Transforming Document Management at KMRL

Since commencing operations in 2017, Kochi Metro Rail Limited (KMRL) has evolved into a complex, multidisciplinary organization that extends far beyond train services. Each day, the enterprise manages thousands of documents spanning engineering, procurement, safety, compliance, HR, and finance.

The Challenge

The growing scale and diversity of this information present challenges in timeliness, accuracy, and institutional knowledge retention. As KMRL expands with new corridors, depots, and digital technologies, these challenges intensify, requiring innovative solutions to maintain operational excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance operational efficiency, safeguard compliance, and deliver safe, sustainable, and passenger-centric urban mobility for Kochi. We focus on equipping every stakeholder with reliable, traceable, and actionable information to support KMRL's growth and technological advancement.
